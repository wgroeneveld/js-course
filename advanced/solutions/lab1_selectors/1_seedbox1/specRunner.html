Lab 1: Gardening in the Seedbox

Since you are all now certified Gardening Masters © - you may now buy and manage your own seeds.
This is what you'll need to do:

  • At the left you can see your seedbox. This is where you'll need to manage your seeds
  • You need to generate a set amount of seeds (the only argument to the function). This will have to be implemented in 'generateSeeds()'
  • Each seed should create a corresponding 'seed' div (with the css class 'seed' on it), within the seedbox div.
  • Next to the seed class, every seed should have one of the following classes randomly:
    1. peashooter
    2. pumpkin
    3. cherrybomb
  • Don't forget to cleanup your seeds. We call 'cleanupSeeds' after each test.
