Lab 1: Twitter client - the Zombified version!

Take out your twitter wall solution from lab 1. Do you remember the boring avatars we gave you? Yeah, those.
Well, let's make 'em better by zombifying them while hovering.

  • If you hover your mouse over an avatar, the avatar changes into a zombified version (just change the src of the image to res/zombie.png)
  • If you hover away from the avatar, the avater changes back to normal.

Remember to use closure environments to capture the needed data!
