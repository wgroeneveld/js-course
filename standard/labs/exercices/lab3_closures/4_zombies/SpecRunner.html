Lab 3: Zombies came to eat your plants!

Oh no, a massive army of zombies came to eat all your carefully grown plants! Let's 

reduce()
them to dust. (got that? *wink*)

The zombie object skeleton has already been provided (see zombies.js). As each zombie differs in strength, reducing them creates a certain amount of dust. This amount is expressed in a simple value.
Stronger zombies will produce more "dust" - multiply the strength factor by 10 to get the dust amount.


This is the public interface: 

ZombieArmy = { // predefined zombie army with varying strength
  zombie,
  coneheadZombie, 
  ...
};

// Notice that biteTheDust takes in an array.
var amountOfDust = biteTheDust([ZombieArmy.zombie, ZombieArmy.coneheadZombie, ZombieArmy.poleVaultingZombie]);
// amount = each zombie's strength multiplied by 10.

Tips:

  • use 'ZombieArmy.enlist' to create one big fat ugly looking zombie and simply execute the calculation on him.
  • Do not use any kind of looping! This may come in handy.
